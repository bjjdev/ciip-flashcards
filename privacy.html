Privacy Policy
CIIP Flashcards  ·  Effective May 3, 2026

What We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the Service:

  • Email address — used to create your account and send transactional emails (e.g. password reset).
  • Study progress data — your card ratings, review history, and settings, stored so your progress syncs across sessions.
  • Payment information — processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number. We store only your Stripe customer ID and subscription status.

We do not use analytics trackers, advertising pixels, or third-party tracking scripts.

How We Use It

  • To authenticate you and manage your account.
  • To sync your study progress across devices.
  • To process subscription payments and manage billing.
  • To send transactional emails (account-related only — no marketing).

Third-Party Services

We use two third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Supabase — stores your account data and study progress. Data is hosted on Supabase-managed infrastructure. See Supabase's privacy policy.
  • Stripe — processes subscription payments. Your payment data is governed by Stripe's privacy policy.

Data Retention

Your account and study data are retained for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, your data will be deleted within 30 days. Billing records may be retained longer as required by law.

Your Rights

You can request a copy of your data, correction of inaccurate data, or deletion of your account at any time by emailing riosoman@proton.me.

Security

Access to your data is protected by authentication and row-level security policies. Payment data never touches our servers — it goes directly to Stripe. We take reasonable steps to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you by email or by a notice on the site before the changes take effect.

Contact

Questions or requests: riosoman@proton.me
